🔹 Step 2: Upload Your Website Files

  1. On your repo page, click “Add file” → “Upload files”

  2. Upload:

    • index.html

    • style.css

  3. After uploading, click “Commit changes”

🔹 Step 3: Enable GitHub Pages

  1. Go to Settings tab of your repo

  2. On the left, click “Pages”

  3. Under "Source", select:

    • Branch: main

    • Folder: /root

  4. Click Save

After a few seconds, GitHub will show a green box with your site URL:

Your site is live at: https://yourusername.github.io

🎉 Boom! Your website is now public.
